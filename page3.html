Why was it theorized?

The 5 elements of nature was an explanation provided for the smallest unit of matter. Basically, people tried to break down matter into pieces and realized that matter kept dividing but there had to be a point after which matter is undivisible. Since, the technology to prove the existance of atoms wasn't there during the 4th century, Aristotle and Zou Yan claimed matter to be made of 5 fundamental elements.
