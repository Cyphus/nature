Elements Of Nature

According to Zou Yan (305-240 BC) and Aristotle (384-322 BC), matter was composed of 5 elements, namely - fire, air, water, earth. Aristotle believed that they were capable of being transformed into one another, just matter can be converted from form to form. The chinese called this "Wuxing" and the elements "Wu". The Wuxing Theory states that there are 5 material elements on earth, namely - wood, fire, earth, metal and water. These elements are the fundamental components of all material things on Earth.
